Livestock Sales & Services Wheatland, CA

AG Farmstead is a modest 40-acre farm located in Wheatland, California, just outside of Sacramento. Our passion for animal raising stemmed from our traditions of cultivating the land around us to keep our animals grass-fed and free of antibiotics and growth hormones.

Our lively goats, sheep, and chickens are raised on our humble farm, equipped with paddocks and shaded shelters to protect them from drafts, high heat, and rain. The natural green environment allows our herd the freedom to roam, graze and browse our pastures and Chase, our spirited Pitbull keeps them nimble with plenty of exercises!

Please feel free to call or email us for any inquiries. We will be happy to assist you.
